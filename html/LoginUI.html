Life is but a journey...We live and learn. This is essential to growth. I believe that our nature as human beings is to give back to the world we were created in using our god given abilities that are externally shown through our personalities. What if there was a test that could determine who you are and what types of "powers" and contributions you can bring to society. Well there is! Although, it is soley your choice to beileve in the accuracy of a test called MBTI. Test are a great means of measurements to some and shallow to others. Some may say that this is shallow way of categorizing people and putting them in a box, a very great point. In my personal opinion it is best to take note of your results, research a bit from there. If the things you are reading do not reasonate with you you can just move on with your life. The root of MBTI stems from the cognitive functions. The cognitive functions are a conceived model by Carl Jung who is also known for concepts like the collective unconcious, introversion and extroversion. Throught the years The concepts of the cognitive functions have been watered down mainly because it is such a broad topic to research. MBTI is indeed a watered down version for the Jungarioan philosophy but it is the most popular place for people to start and the easiest to understand. This website is suppose to be a place where users have to own profile that will provide them with informative resources based on their personality type.
